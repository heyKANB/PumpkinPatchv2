Privacy Policy for Pumpkin Patch

Effective Date: August 14, 2025

Last Updated: August 14, 2025

Quick Summary: This Pumpkin Patch farming game respects your privacy. We only collect minimal data necessary for ads and game functionality. No personal information is stored or shared.

1. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

  • Game Statistics: Farm progress, pumpkin harvest counts, and game preferences (stored locally on your device)
  • Advertising Data: Anonymous identifiers for ad personalization through integrated ad services
  • Technical Data: Device type, operating system, app version for crash reporting and performance optimization
  • Gameplay Analytics: Anonymous farming actions and game completion metrics to improve the gaming experience

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Microphone or camera access
  • Real financial information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Game Experience: Save your farm progress and preferences locally
  • Advertisements: Display relevant ads through integrated advertising services
  • App Improvement: Analyze crash reports and performance metrics
  • Gameplay Enhancement: Optimize farming mechanics and user interface

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Game data is stored locally on your device
  • No Account Required: No user accounts or cloud storage
  • Security: Standard encryption for any data transmission
  • Data Retention: Game progress saved until app is uninstalled

4. Third-Party Services

Advertising Services

We may use third-party advertising services to display advertisements. These services may collect and use data according to their privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdSense Privacy: https://support.google.com/adsense/answer/48182

Analytics

We may use analytics services to understand how users interact with our farming game to improve the experience.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and designed as a family-friendly farming simulation. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game operates without requiring personal data input.

6. Your Choices

  • Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings or the App Tracking Transparency prompt
  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored farm data
  • Sound/Theme Preferences: Adjustable within the game settings
  • Tracking Consent: iOS users can manage tracking preferences through system settings

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last Modified" date.

8. International Users

This app complies with:

  • GDPR (European Union)
  • CCPA (California)
  • COPPA (Children's privacy)
  • App Tracking Transparency (iOS)
